### Masalah Formatting yang Ditemukan

User menemukan bahwa dokumen BAB_1_DAN_2.md memiliki **terlalu banyak whitespace** (ruang kosong) dalam rendering karena banyak paragraf yang terpotong-potong dengan line breaks di tengah kalimat. Ini disebabkan oleh proses konversi dari Word (pandoc) yang mempertahankan word wrapping dengan lebar tertentu.

**1. Menggabungkan Baris-Baris dalam Paragraf**

## [2025-12-18] - Penambahan Research Gap dan Novelty (Update 6)

### Penambahan Bagian Kritis untuk Justifikasi Penelitian

**Masalah:** BAB 1 belum secara eksplisit menunjukkan **research gap** dan
**novelty/kontribusi penelitian** yang jelas dan terstruktur.

**Solusi:** Menambahkan 2 bagian baru yang komprehensif setelah Latar
Belakang:

### A. Research Gap (Kesenjangan Penelitian) - 4 Gap Utama:

**Gap 1: Limited Systematic Comparison of Imbalance Handling Strategies**
- Most studies use single strategy atau ignore imbalance issue
- No comprehensive comparison: Original, Class Weights, SMOTE, ADASYN,
  Hybrid pada actigraphy depression data
- Impact: Choice dapat change performance 15-25% (López et al., 2013)

**Gap 2: Insufficient Algorithm Comparison on Actigraphy Data**
- DT vs GNB memiliki fundamental differences
- No direct head-to-head comparison pada actigraphy depression data
- Existing comparisons: activity recognition (Maswadi) atau general medical
  (Ramadhani), bukan depression time series

**Gap 3: Lack of Feature-Level Interpretation for Clinical Translation**
- High accuracy tetapi tidak explore "which features discriminate"
- Burton et al. (2013) identify patterns tetapi tidak quantify exact
  features
- Models remain "black box" untuk clinical translation

**Gap 4: Algorithm-Strategy Interaction Effects Unexplored**
- Strategies may interact differently dengan algorithms
- Batista et al. (2004) hint interaction tetapi limited scope
- No empirical mapping untuk optimal combinations

### B. Novelty dan Kontribusi Penelitian - 5 Novelties:

**Novelty 1: Systematic Multi-Strategy Comparison Framework**
- First comprehensive study: 5 strategies × 2 algorithms = 10 models
- Rigorous CV + statistical testing (Friedman, Wilcoxon)
- Evidence-based guidelines: ADASYN optimal untuk DT (F1: 0.88)

**Novelty 2: Algorithm-Specific Performance Analysis**
- Direct empirical comparison dengan biological validation
- Explains WHY DT outperforms GNB (~23% gap):
  - Assumption violations (independence, Gaussian)
  - Capability matching (temporal patterns, interactions)

**Novelty 3: Feature-Level Clinical Insights**
- Beyond accuracy: feature importance, temporal patterns, decision rules
- Translational insights:
  - Objective biomarkers: evening activity, circadian strength, day/night
    ratio
  - Quantitative thresholds: activity < 220.5 + weak rhythm → 85%
    depression
  - Monitoring targets: evening hours, circadian regularity

**Novelty 4: Algorithm-Strategy Interaction Mapping**
- Explicit interaction examination:
  - DT benefits substantially (81% → 88%)
  - GNB minimal benefit (<1% variation)
  - Hybrid tidak better than single methods
- Practical decision matrix untuk model selection

**Novelty 5: Methodological Rigor and Reproducibility**
- Best practices: stratified split, pipeline feature selection, nested
  evaluation
- Multiple metrics + statistical testing + biological validation
- Reproducible template untuk future research

### Expected Impact:
1. Scientific: Evidence base untuk algorithm/strategy selection
2. Clinical: Objective biomarkers untuk depression screening
3. Technology: Guidelines untuk ML-based monitoring apps
4. Policy: Supporting wearable-based assessment integration

**Total Konten Ditambahkan:**
- ~2000 kata
- 4 research gaps dengan evidence dan dampak
- 5 novelties dengan kontribusi spesifik
- Structured dan comprehensive justification
